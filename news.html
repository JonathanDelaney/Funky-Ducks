COVID

This year the world was brought to its knees by the challenge that has been the pandemic. It has disrupted all industries and the music one was no exception. There have been few things to be more grateful for at this time than our health. This doesn't make the plight of our smal/independant artists any less serious. Those involved in transport and set-up and all those whose livelihood depends on things comtinuing as they were are struggling. Please support them by buying tickets!
