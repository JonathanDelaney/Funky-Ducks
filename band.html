Gloria

Gloria in excelsis deo! She is THE guitarist everyone who talks about guitarists is talking about right now. Her nimble fingers make light work of the demanding riffs the Funked Up Chicks have made their staple.

Stevie

The lead singer and bass player is enigmatic off the stage and beguiling under the spotlight. A spell is cast over the audience each time she looks in their direction and they are pushed into a funky trance when she sings.

Donna

Drumstick Donna is a fan of KFC but that is not what earned her the title. Born in New Orleans, she first played drums for a street jazz group at just 15 months old. Her rythmic riches brought her family out of poverty.
